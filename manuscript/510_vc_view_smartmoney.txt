## A founder-CEOs and VC access to superpowers of professional CEOs

Um dos benefícios do empreendedor receber investimento das empresas de capital de risco, é quando eles tem também a oportunidade de acessar suas redes de contato e recursos. Para Marc Andreessen, parceiro da Andreessen Horowitz, esta capacidade é um deferencial da sua firma:

```
The other thing that we did, and this is a huge part of the firm today, we built a whole operating model around . . . giving the founder, superpowers. . . and a lot of that has to do with network: the network of all the customers that you are going to sell to, the network of all the later on investors that you are going to raise the money from, the network of all the big industry companies that you have to work with, the of all the reporters to get your story out, the network of all the engineers that you need to recruit, the network of all the executives that you need to recruit, the network of all the policy makers and regulators that you need to deal with. (Y Combinator, 2016, 3:36).
```
